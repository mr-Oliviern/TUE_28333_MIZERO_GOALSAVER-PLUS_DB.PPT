## 1. Overview
The Business Intelligence (BI) component of GoalSaver Plus is designed to transform operational
data from the savings and loan system into meaningful insights for management, administrators,
and auditors.

The BI layer supports monitoring, decision-making, risk management, and regulatory compliance.

---

## 2. BI Objectives
The objectives of the BI system are to:
- Monitor member savings behavior over time
- Evaluate loan performance and repayment trends
- Detect risky or non-compliant activities
- Track system usage and user accountability
- Support data-driven strategic decisions

---

## 3. Key Business Questions
The BI system must answer the following questions:

### Savings & Accounts
- What is the total savings amount in the system?
- How does savings growth change monthly?
- Which account types hold the most funds?
- Which members have declining balances?

### Loans
- How many loans are approved, rejected, or pending?
- What is the total loan amount disbursed?
- What is the outstanding loan balance?
- Which members are high-risk borrowers?

### Compliance & Auditing
- How many restricted transactions were attempted?
- Which users frequently violate business rules?
- On which days do most failed operations occur?
- Are audit controls being enforced effectively?

---

## 4. Data Sources
The BI system uses data from the following tables:
- ACCOUNT
- ACCOUNT_TRANSACTION
- LOAN
- MEMBER
- AUDIT_LOG
- PUBLIC_HOLIDAYS

---

## 5. BI Users
- System Administrators
- Finance Managers
- Loan Officers
- Auditors
- Executive Management

---

## 6. Expected Benefits
- Improved transparency in financial operations
- Reduced financial and compliance risk
- Faster and better decision-making
- Strong audit trail and accountability
