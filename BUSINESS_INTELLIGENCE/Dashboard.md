## 1. Savings Overview Dashboard
Purpose: Provide a high-level view of savings performance.

### Metrics Displayed:
- Total savings balance
- Average savings per member
- Savings growth trend (monthly)
- Savings by account type

### Visuals:
- Line chart (savings growth)
- Bar chart (account types)
- KPI cards (totals)

---

## 2. Loan Management Dashboard
Purpose: Monitor loan issuance and risk.

### Metrics Displayed:
- Total loans disbursed
- Loan approval vs rejection
- Outstanding loan balance
- High-risk borrowers

### Visuals:
- Pie chart (loan status)
- Bar chart (loan amounts)
- Table (top borrowers)

---

## 3. Compliance & Audit Dashboard
Purpose: Ensure system security and rule enforcement.

### Metrics Displayed:
- Failed vs successful operations
- Restricted transaction attempts
- User activity levels
- Audit events timeline

### Visuals:
- Bar chart (failed actions by user)
- Line chart (audit activity over time)
- Table (audit log summary)

---

## 4. Executive Summary Dashboard
Purpose: Provide management with strategic insights.

### Metrics Displayed:
- Total savings
- Total loans
- Compliance status
- System activity overview

### Visuals:
- KPI scorecards
- Trend indicators
